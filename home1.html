History of Violy's SARI-SARI Store

The store was founded in the year 1990 as a small family business. It began as a simple sari-sari store built beside the family home, made to serve neighbors with everyday items like snacks, drinks, and basic household needs.

As the years passed, more customers started coming. Around 2005, the store slowly expanded and added more products to meet the needs of the community.

By 2015, the store had become well-known in the neighborhood. It started to look more like a convenience store, offering a wider variety of items and providing better service.

Today, the store continues to serve the community with affordable products and a warm, friendly atmosphere. What began as a small family idea in 2000 has grown into a trusted place where people can find their daily needs.
